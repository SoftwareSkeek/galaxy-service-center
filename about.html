The Repairing Experts You’ve Trusted For Over 20 Years.

At Galaxy Service Center, we are dedicated to providing top-quality appliance repair services to our valued customers. With years of experience in the industry, our team of skilled technicians has earned a reputation for reliability, professionalism, and excellence in service.

Our mission is simple: to keep your household running smoothly by ensuring that your appliances are in optimal working condition. Whether it's a refrigerator, washing machine, oven, dishwasher, or dryer, we have the expertise to diagnose and fix a wide range of appliance issues.

What sets us apart is our commitment to customer satisfaction. We understand the inconvenience of a malfunctioning appliance, which is why we prioritize prompt service, transparent pricing, and quality workmanship. Our goal is to exceed your expectations with every repair we undertake.
